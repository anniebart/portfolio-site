about me

hi, i'm annie. i am a designer, programmer/developer and artist. i was a member of crit in the fall of 2018 and i am an alum of recurse center.

i got my BA in studio art and environmental studies from middlebury college in 2017. i've previously worked at the whitney museum (2017, 2018), third eye (2017-2018), and APPARATUS (2018-2020). currently, i am a senior digital content fellow at our climate voices, a climate justice organization. i've also done freelance design work for clients in the media + tech fields. if you are interested in collaborating, you can email me.

i currently live and work in brooklyn.
